隐私政策

生效日期：2026-07-03 · Effective date: July 3, 2026

中文在前，English version below ↓

简体中文

一句话总结：书签管理器 Pro（下称"本扩展"）是一个纯本地工具。我们没有服务器、没有账号系统，不收集、不上传、不出售你的任何数据。

1. 我们收集哪些数据

不收集。本扩展没有任何后端服务器，开发者无法接触到你的任何数据。以下信息仅保存在你自己浏览器的本地存储（chrome.storage.local）中，用于实现功能：

  • 你手动设置的书签分类；
  • 在本扩展中打开书签的次数与时间（用于"常用书签"排序）；
  • 界面偏好：主题、显示密度、排序方式、是否显示网页预览。

书签本身由 Chrome 浏览器管理，本扩展只是读取和（在你操作时）修改它们。卸载本扩展后，上述本地数据会被 Chrome 自动清除，你的书签不受影响。

2. 权限用途逐项说明

权限用途
bookmarks读取和管理你的 Chrome 书签——这是本扩展的核心功能（展示、搜索、移动、编辑、删除、排序）。
storage在本地保存你的分类和界面偏好（见第 1 条）。
history读取最近 30 天的访问次数，在本地计算"常用书签"排序。历史记录不会离开你的浏览器。
favicon从 Chrome 本地的图标缓存读取网站图标用于展示，零网络请求。
s0.wp.com获取网页预览截图（见第 3 条），可关闭。
可选：所有网站仅当你主动点击"检测失效链接"时，扩展会请求此权限，用于逐个访问你的书签网址以检查其是否仍然有效。不点击该功能，此权限不会被申请。

3. 第三方服务：网页预览截图

预览网格中的网站截图由 WordPress.com 的公开截图服务（mShots，域名 s0.wp.com，由 Automattic 公司运营）生成。启用该功能时，你的书签网址会被发送给该服务以生成对应截图；截图结果缓存在你的浏览器本地。该服务受 Automattic 隐私政策约束。

如果你不希望发送任何书签网址：在导航页「更多」菜单中点击「隐藏网页预览」即可完全关闭此功能，此后本扩展不会发出任何外部网络请求。

4. 我们不做的事

  • 不收集、不上传、不出售、不共享任何个人数据；
  • 不接入任何统计 / 分析 / 广告 SDK；
  • 不要求注册账号，不设置任何跟踪标识。

5. 数据删除

卸载本扩展即删除其全部本地数据（Chrome 自动完成）。你的书签是 Chrome 的数据，不会因卸载本扩展而改变。

6. 政策变更与联系方式

如本政策有实质性变更，我们会在扩展更新说明和本页面中注明。任何疑问请联系：allenma2ong@gmail.com

ENGLISH

In one sentence: Bookmark Manager Pro ("the Extension") is a local-only tool. There are no servers and no accounts; we do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.

1. What data we collect

None. The Extension has no backend, and the developer has no access to your data. The following is stored only in your own browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) to make features work:

  • Categories you assign to bookmarks manually;
  • How often and when you open bookmarks through the Extension (powers "Frequent" sorting);
  • Interface preferences: theme, display density, sort order, preview on/off.

Your bookmarks themselves are managed by Chrome; the Extension only reads them and modifies them when you act. Uninstalling the Extension removes all of the local data above (handled by Chrome); your bookmarks are unaffected.

2. Permissions, one by one

PermissionWhy it's needed
bookmarksRead and manage your Chrome bookmarks — the core purpose of the Extension (display, search, move, edit, delete, reorder).
storageSave your categories and preferences locally (see section 1).
historyRead visit counts from the last 30 days to rank "Frequent" bookmarks, computed locally. Your history never leaves your browser.
faviconShow site icons from Chrome's local favicon cache — zero network requests.
s0.wp.comFetch preview screenshots (see section 3). Can be turned off.
Optional: all sitesRequested only when you click "Check dead links", so the Extension can request each bookmarked URL to verify it still works. If you never use that feature, this permission is never requested.

3. Third-party service: preview screenshots

Website screenshots in the preview grid are generated by WordPress.com's public screenshot service (mShots, domain s0.wp.com, operated by Automattic). When this feature is on, your bookmark URLs are sent to that service to generate screenshots; results are cached locally in your browser. The service is governed by Automattic's privacy policy.

If you prefer not to send any bookmark URLs: choose "Hide Page Previews" in the "More" menu. After that, the Extension makes no external network requests at all.

4. What we never do

  • No collecting, uploading, selling, or sharing of personal data;
  • No analytics, telemetry, or advertising SDKs;
  • No accounts, no tracking identifiers.

5. Data deletion

Uninstalling the Extension deletes all of its local data (Chrome handles this automatically). Your bookmarks belong to Chrome and are not affected.

6. Changes & contact

Material changes to this policy will be noted in the extension's release notes and on this page. Questions: allenma2ong@gmail.com
